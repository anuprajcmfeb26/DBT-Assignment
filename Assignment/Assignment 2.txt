1) Which field of the Customers table is the primary key?

The primary key of the Customers table is Cnum (Customer Number) because it uniquely identifies each customer.

2) What is the 4th column of the Customers table?

The 4th column of the Customers table is City.

3) What is another word for row? For column?

Another word for row is record.

Another word for column is field.

4) Why isn’t it possible to see the first five rows of a table?

Because tables in a relational database do not have a fixed order of rows.
Rows can appear in any order unless we use an ORDER BY clause in a query.
